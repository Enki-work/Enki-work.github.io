Features

Everything you need to
look great on camera

Designed for creators who want professional results without the professional crew.

📜

Teleprompter Overlay

Your script floats transparently over the live camera preview. Adjustable frosted-glass blur (0–100%) keeps your footage clearly visible behind the text.

🔘

Hands-Free Page Flip

Advance through your script with the volume button (+/−), a Bluetooth remote shutter, or the keyboard — no screen tapping needed.

🎥

4K HDR Recording

Record up to 4K (3840×2160) with HEVC + HLG BT.2020 wide-color HDR, or H.264 SDR for universal compatibility.

🔭

All iPhone Lenses

Switch freely between wide, ultra-wide, and telephoto lenses. Choose your preferred format and frame rate with a single tap.

🎨

Fully Customizable

Pick any background and text color, adjust font size (12–60 pt), and fine-tune blur intensity — all with live preview.

💾

Auto-Save Everything

Your script and all appearance settings are saved automatically. Pick up exactly where you left off every time you open the app.

How it works

Shoot professional videos in 3 easy steps

1

Type your script

Enter your script in the app before you shoot. Organize it into pages for easy navigation.

2

Set up & customize

Choose your lens, resolution, and frame rate. Adjust the teleprompter appearance to match your lighting and style.

3

Hit record & read naturally

Press record and start reading. Flip pages with the volume button or Bluetooth remote — your video is saved automatically to Photos when done.
